Uzumaki Naruto

Naruto is a young ninja who dreams of becoming the Hokage, the leader of his village.
He is known for his boundless energy, mischievous pranks, and his ability to make unexpected friends
As the series progresses, Naruto grows stronger both physically and mentally, mastering various ninja techniques and forming strong bonds with his teammates and friends. His unwavering determination and positive attitude inspire those around him, making him a beloved character in the Naruto universe.

Uzumaki Naruto

Naruto was born on October 10th to Minato Namikaze and Kushina Uzumaki

As a newborn, Naruto was made the jinchuriki of the Nine-Tails fox after the beast attacked Konoha

This caused him to be shunned by many villagers throughout his childhood

Despite the hardships, Naruto dreamed of becoming the Hokage to gain the village's acknowledgement.
